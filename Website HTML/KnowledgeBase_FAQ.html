Knowledge Base / FAQ

LifeBand is a smart wearable device designed to protect individuals living with epilepsy. It provides real-time monitoring, instant emergency alerts, and GPS tracking — helping patients stay safe while living more independently. Built using accessible technology, LifeBand offers a low-cost alternative to expensive hospital-based seizure monitoring systems.

    Key Features

  • Real-Time Seizure Detection

    • Monitors unusual body movements and abnormal heart rate patterns associated with seizure activity.

  • Emergency Alerts

    • Automatically sends notifications to caregivers through a connected mobile application.

  • GPS Location Tracking

    • Provides real-time location during emergency alerts so help can arrive faster.

  • Heart Rate Monitoring

    • Displays live heart rate readings and tracks irregular changes.

  • Seizure Log & Medical Tracking

    • Records seizure events including:

    • Date
    • Time
    • Duration
    • Movement intensity

    This will help caregivers and doctors monitor patterns over time.

  • Medication Reminder

    • Sends scheduled reminders to ensure medications are taken on time.

  • Smart Alert Cancellation

    • Includes a 10-second countdown to prevent false alarms before emergency notifications are sent.

    LifeBand is designed as a low-cost, portable solution using widely available components. Unlike traditional medical-grade monitoring equipment, which can be expensive and hospital-based, LifeBand focuses on everyday use at home or outdoors. It promotes Greater independence, Continuous safety support, and Reduced need for constant supervision

    LifeBand is very simple to use. just follow these 5 steps!

    1. Wear It

  • Secure the LifeBand on your wrist comfortably.

    • 2. Connect to the App

  • Pair the device with the LifeBand mobile application via Bluetooth

    • 3. Stay Protected

  • The device automatically monitors your condition throughout the day.

    • 4. Receive Alerts

  • If abnormal patterns are detected, The wearer receives vibration feedback, Caregivers receive instant notifications, and GPS location is shared for immediate assistance

    • 5. Review Health Data

  • The wearer or the caregivers can use the app to view the wearer's heart rate, seizure logs, and monitor the medication schedules.
