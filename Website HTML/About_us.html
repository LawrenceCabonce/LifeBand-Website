ABOUT US

We are Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) students from NU Fairview, driven by one shared goal — to use technology to solve real-world problems that truly matter.

LifeBand was created not just as a school project, but as a response to a real and urgent concern faced by individuals living with epilepsy. We recognized how sudden seizures can affect independence, safety, and overall quality of life. Instead of viewing this as just a technical challenge, we saw it as an opportunity to build something meaningful and impactful.

As future IT professionals, we believe technology should go beyond coding and hardware — it should empower people, promote safety, and create inclusive solutions for communities that need support the most.

Our team is passionate about innovation, accessibility, and social responsibility. Through LifeBand, we aim to demonstrate how information technology can bridge the gap between healthcare challenges and practical, affordable solutions.
